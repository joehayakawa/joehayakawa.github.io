📅 Day-by-Day Itinerary

Cathy's Original — Highlands Loop

Day 1 — 23 May
Almaty, Kazakhstan
  • Arrive at midnight, sleep in
  • Green Bazaar — dried fruits, nuts, spices
  • Panfilov Park & wooden cathedral (1907)
Days 2–3 — 24–25 May
Bishkek & Ala Archa National Park
  • 4–5hr border crossing to Bishkek
  • Ala Archa: glacier valley, Ak-Sai viewpoint
  • 16km hike, 800m elevation gain
Day 4 — 26 May
Song-Kul Plateau (3,016m) ⚠️
  • Nomadic yurt camp on alpine lake
  • Horse riding on the steppe
  • Fermented mare's milk, zero light pollution
  • ⚠️ Road may still be closed in May
Day 5 — 27 May
Bokonbaevo & Issyk-Kul
  • Eagle hunting demo (UNESCO heritage)
  • South shore of Issyk-Kul lake
  • 182km long, never freezes
Day 6 — 28 May
Karakol
  • Trekking base camp city
  • Dungan Mosque (Chinese-style)
  • Russian Orthodox timber church
  • Karakol Gorge afternoon hike
Day 7 — 29 May
Jyrgalan Valley
  • Echki-Jailoo loop — 18km hike
  • Alpine meadows, wildflowers, marmots
  • Ethical horse riding with local families
Day 8 — 30 May
Altyn Arashan ✨
  • "Golden Spa" — NON-NEGOTIABLE
  • Natural hot springs at 2,900m
  • Glacier peak views (4,000m+)
  • Yurt camp overnight, stars at altitude
Days 9–10 — 31 May – 1 Jun
Return: Bishkek → Almaty → ✈️
  • Hike down from Altyn Arashan
  • Drive to Bishkek, cross border
  • Final exploration in Almaty
  • Evening flights home

Which One is Right for You?

Three solid plans, each with a different character. Your choice depends on how much you value independence vs. convenience, and hiking depth vs. cultural breadth.

Choose Cathy's Original if you want...

The most personally curated route with the best hiking trails (Jyrgalan, Altyn Arashan, Ala Archa), maximum flexibility, and the freedom to shape the trip as you go — but you'll need to handle all logistics yourselves.

Choose Agent 1 if you want...

A comprehensive, all-inclusive grand tour covering both countries with rich cultural experiences, iconic landmarks, and a well-defined itinerary with no planning left to chance.

Choose Agent 2 if you want...

A budget-friendly, guided hiking adventure in Kyrgyzstan with flexible intensity, more time in nature, yurt stays, horse riding, and the freedom to push your limits on the trail.
